Manifestation System

Form / Damage / Control

Base, Rage and Incubus states change Ryo's portrait and the Archive's presence. Damage changes what the Archive reveals about his condition.

  • Base shows Ryo when he is controlled and himself.
  • Rage reveals the draconic side through pressure and force.
  • Incubus emergence shows inherited instinct and loss of control.
  • Collapse is shared as the 0 HP state.
